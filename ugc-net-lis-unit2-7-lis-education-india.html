🕐Timeline — India में LIS Education

1887 (World)

🌍 World's First Library School

Melvil Dewey — Columbia University, New York, USA। School of Library Economy। विश्व का पहला Formal Library School।

1911

🇮🇳 India का पहला LIS Training Course

W.A. Borden — Baroda (Vadodara, Gujarat)। Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad III के आग्रह पर। India का पहला Library Training Program।

1915

🏫 पहला University LIS Degree Course

Punjab University (Lahore) — A.D. Dickinson। India का पहला University-level Library Science Degree।

1929

🏛️ Delhi Library School

A.D. Dickinson — Delhi। बाद में Delhi University Department of Library Science बना।

1930s–1950s

📚 Ranganathan Era — Madras

S.R. Ranganathan ने Madras University में Full-fledged LIS Department बनाया। Five Laws (1931) Curriculum में। Post-Independence Expansion।

1962

🔬 DRTC की स्थापना — Bangalore

S.R. Ranganathan — Documentation Research and Training Centre, Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore। Advanced LIS Research + Training।

1989

📝 UGC NET in Library Science शुरू

UGC ने LIS में NET शुरू किया। University Teaching और JRF के लिए National Eligibility Test। Standard Qualification Established।

2001–Present

💻 Digital Age — CBCS, NEP 2020

IT Integration in LIS। IGNOU Distance Programs। CBCS (2015)। NEP 2020 = MPhil Discontinued, 4-year UG। Online Education Boom।

UGC NET Eligibility

UGC NET for LIS: 55% in MLISc (General/OBC-NCL)। 50% (SC/ST/PwD)। JRF Age: Max 30 years। Assistant Professor: No Age Limit। NET = National level test for Teaching + JRF.

❓FAQs — 10 महत्वपूर्ण प्रश्न

Q1. भारत में पहला Library Science Course कब और कहाँ शुरू हुआ?+
India का पहला LIS Course = 1911, Baroda (Vadodara, Gujarat)। W.A. Borden (American Expert)। Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad III के आग्रह पर। 1910 = Library System Setup। 1911 = Training Course। World's First = 1887, Columbia University, Melvil Dewey।
Q2. BLISc और MLISc में क्या अंतर है? UGC NET के लिए क्या चाहिए?+
BLISc = 1 Year, Any Graduate, Library Jobs। MLISc = 1-2 Years, BLISc 55%, Senior Posts। UGC NET = MLISc में 55% (General) / 50% (Reserved)। JRF Age Max = 30 yrs। Assistant Professor = No Age Limit।
Q3. DRTC क्या है? इसे किसने Founded किया?+
DRTC = Documentation Research and Training Centre। Founded by S.R. Ranganathan, 1962। Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore। Advanced LIS Research + Training। Annual DRTC Seminar = Major LIS Event।
Q4. NEP 2020 ने LIS Education में क्या बदला?+
NEP 2020 Changes: (1) MPhil Discontinued। (2) 4-year UG Honours। (3) Multiple Entry-Exit (Certificate → Diploma → Degree → Honours)। (4) Academic Bank of Credits (ABC)। (5) Multidisciplinary। (6) Internship Mandatory। (7) Indian Languages Medium।
Q5. IGNOU में LIS के कौन से Programs हैं?+
IGNOU Programs: BLISc (1 Yr Distance), MLISc (1 Yr after BLISc), Certificate in LIS, Certificate in Archives. India का सबसे बड़ा Distance LIS Provider। UGC Recognized। UGC NET Eligible।
Q6. A.D. Dickinson का LIS Education में क्या योगदान था?+
A.D. Dickinson (American Librarian): 1915 = Punjab University में पहला University Degree Course। 1929 = Delhi Library School → Delhi University LIS Dept। India में LIS को Academic Discipline बनाया।
Q7. CBCS और LOCF क्या हैं?+
CBCS = Choice Based Credit System (UGC, 2015)। Core + Elective + Skill Enhancement Courses। Credit System। LOCF = Learning Outcomes Based Curriculum Framework (UGC, 2019)। Graduate Attributes, PO, CO Define करता है। LIS LOCF भी है।
Q8. Shodhganga का LIS Education से क्या सम्बन्ध है?+
Shodhganga = INFLIBNET (UGC), Ahmedabad। National ETD Repository (Electronic Theses)। 7 Lakh+ PhD Theses। UGC Mandate = PhD Scholars को Thesis यहाँ Submit करना अनिवार्य। LIS PhD Theses भी यहाँ। Free Access। DSpace Platform।
Q9. LIS Curriculum में कौन से Core Areas हैं?+
UGC LIS Curriculum Core Areas: (1) Foundation = Five Laws, LIS History, Info Science। (2) Knowledge Organisation = Classification (DDC/UDC/CC), Cataloguing (AACR2/RDA)। (3) Information Sources & Services = Reference, CAS/SDI, IL। (4) Technology = Library Automation, Digital Libraries। (5) Management = Library Admin, Finance, HRM। (6) Research = Bibliometrics, Research Methods।
Q10. LIS Education में Emerging Trends क्या हैं?+
Emerging Trends: (1) AI/ML = Chatbots, NLP Cataloguing। (2) Data Science = FAIR Data, Bibliometrics। (3) Linked Data = BIBFRAME, Ontologies। (4) Open Access = IR, Preprints। (5) Information Security = DPDPA 2023। (6) Accessibility = DAISY, Marrakesh Treaty 2013। (7) Interdisciplinary = LIS + CS, LIS + Management।
