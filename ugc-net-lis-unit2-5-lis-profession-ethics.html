LIS एक Profession क्यों है? LIS (Library & Information Science) एक Profession है क्योंकि इसमें सभी 6 Criteria पूरे होते हैं: (1) Systematic Knowledge Body, (2) Formal Education (BLISc, MLISc), (3) Professional Associations (ILA, ALA, IFLA), (4) Code of Ethics (IFLA 2012), (5) Service Orientation, (6) Autonomy & Self-regulation। IFLA Code of Ethics = 2012। Core Values: Free Access, Intellectual Freedom, Privacy, Service, Democracy। New Roles: Knowledge Manager, Digital Librarian, Information Architect।

Librarianship — ग्रंथालय विज्ञान व्यवसाय

Custodian of Books → Information Professional → Knowledge Manager → Digital Librarian
Evolving Profession

Librarianship = वह Profession जो Information/Knowledge के Acquisition, Organisation, Preservation और Dissemination से सम्बंधित है — Users की Needs को पूरा करने के लिए। यह समय के साथ Custodial Role से Information Professional Role में विकसित हुई है।

Librarianship का विकास — 4 Stages

🏛️
Stage 1: Custodian
Books की Custody। Preservation Focus। Restricted Access। Pre-20th Century।
📚
Stage 2: Service Provider
Ranganathan's Era। Open Access। User-centred। 20th Century।
💻
Stage 3: Info Professional
IT + LIS। Database, OPAC, Internet। Late 20th Century।
🤖
Stage 4: Knowledge Manager
AI + Data + Digital. 21st Century। Embedded Librarian।

⚖️IFLA Code of Ethics — 2012

⚖️

IFLA Code of Ethics for Librarians and Information Workers, 2012

Adopted by IFLA General Assembly | August 2012 | Helsinki, Finland
International Standard

IFLA ने 2012 में Code of Ethics for Librarians and Information Workers अपनाया। यह Code International Level पर LIS Professionals के लिए एक Ethical Framework है।

IFLA Code के 7 Core Principles

1
Access to Information
सूचना तक Free, Equal और Open Access। सभी Citizens को बिना Discrimination के। Ranganathan Law 1 & 2 के अनुरूप।
2
Responsibilities towards Individuals and Society
Users और Society दोनों के प्रति Responsibility। Public Good के लिए काम। Community Service Ethic।
3
Privacy and Data Protection
Borrower Records Confidential। Reading Habits Private। No surveillance of library users। DPDPA alignment।
4
Open Access and Intellectual Property
Open Access Promote करना। Copyright का सम्मान। Balanced approach — Access vs Protection। Open Access Advocacy।
5
Neutrality, Personal Integrity and Professional Skills
No Bias in Collection। Political/Religious Neutrality। Personal Integrity। Continuous Professional Development।
6
Colleagues and the Profession
Peer Respect। Professional Community Support। Fair Treatment of Colleagues। Mentoring Junior Professionals।
7
Employers and Clients
Employers के प्रति Loyalty। Client Confidentiality। Professional Judgment — Employer Instructions के विरुद्ध भी यदि Ethics की माँग हो।
UGC NET Tip IFLA Code = 2012, Helsinki। 7 Principles: A-R-P-O-N-C-E = Access, Responsibilities, Privacy, Open Access, Neutrality, Colleagues, Employers। Privacy = Borrower Records Government को भी नहीं देना। Neutrality = No Political/Religious Bias in Collection।

Intellectual Freedom — Library का Core Value

Intellectual Freedom = किसी भी Idea, Opinion, Information तक Access करने का Right — बिना Government या Institutional Censorship के। यह UN Human Rights Declaration, Article 19 में Guaranteed है। Library में: All viewpoints की Books रखना। Banned Books = Challenge to Intellectual Freedom। ALA's Banned Books Week = Censorship के विरुद्ध Annual Campaign। India में: RTI Act (2005) + IT Act (2000) = Information Access का Legal Framework।

21वीं सदी में Librarian की भूमिका Data Scientist, Knowledge Manager और Digital Archivist तक फैल गई है। Continuous Professional Development — UGC NET से लेकर CILIP Chartered Membership तक — इस Evolving Profession में आगे बढ़ने का मार्ग है।

❓FAQs — 10 महत्वपूर्ण प्रश्न

Q1. LIS को Profession क्यों कहते हैं? इसके 6 Criteria क्या हैं?+
LIS Profession के 6 Criteria (K-E-A-E-S-A): (1) Knowledge Body = DDC, Cataloguing, Reference, Information Retrieval — Systematic Knowledge। (2) Formal Education = BLISc, MLISc, PhD — 100+ Universities India में। (3) Professional Associations = ILA (1933), ALA (1876), IFLA (1927)। (4) Code of Ethics = IFLA Code 2012, ILA Code। (5) Service Orientation = Public Good, Free Access, No Discrimination। (6) Autonomy & Self-regulation = Professional Judgment, UGC Standards, PSC Recruitment। LIS इन सभी Criteria को Fully Meet करती है — इसीलिए यह एक Established Profession है।
Q2. IFLA Code of Ethics (2012) के 7 Principles क्या हैं?+
IFLA Code of Ethics (2012) — Adopted at Helsinki। 7 Principles (A-R-P-O-N-C-E): (1) Access = Free, Equal, Open Access। (2) Responsibilities = Individuals + Society। (3) Privacy = Borrower Records Confidential। (4) Open Access = Promote + IP Balance। (5) Neutrality = No Bias + Professional Integrity + Skills। (6) Colleagues = Peer Respect + Mentoring। (7) Employers & Clients = Loyalty + Ethics First। Key: Privacy Principle = Borrower Reading Habits = Strictly Confidential (even from Government)।
Q3. Intellectual Freedom क्या है? Library में इसका क्या महत्व है?+
Intellectual Freedom = किसी भी Idea, Opinion, Information को Seek, Receive, Impart करने का Right — बिना Censorship के। Legal Basis: UN Declaration of Human Rights, Article 19। Library में: (1) सभी Viewpoints की Books रखना। (2) No Censorship of Ideas। (3) Political/Religious Bias से Collection न बनाना। (4) ALA Banned Books Week = Censored Books को Celebrate करना = Censorship विरुद्ध। (5) IFLA Code में Intellectual Freedom = Core Principle। India में: RTI Act (2005) = Government Information Access। IT Act (2000) = Digital Information Access। Libraries को Intellectual Freedom का Guardian बनना चाहिए।
Q4. Librarianship के 4 Evolution Stages क्या हैं?+
Librarianship के 4 Stages: (1) Custodian Stage = Pre-20th Century। Books की Custody + Preservation। Restricted Access। Chain Libraries (Medieval)। (2) Service Provider Stage = Ranganathan Era (1930s+)। Open Access। User-centred। Five Laws (1931)। (3) Information Professional Stage = Late 20th Century। IT + LIS। OPAC, Online Databases। SDI, CAS Services। (4) Knowledge Manager Stage = 21st Century। AI, Data, Digital। Knowledge Management। Embedded Librarian। Data Librarian। Pioneers: Melvil Dewey (USA) = "Father of Modern Librarianship"। First Library School = 1887। Ranganathan (India) = "Father of Library Science India"। DRTC, Five Laws।
Q5. Core Values of Librarianship क्या हैं? ALA ने कब Define किए?+
ALA ने 2004 में Librarianship के Core Values Define किए। 6 Core Values: (1) Access = Free, Equal, Universal Access। (2) Intellectual Freedom = No Censorship, All Viewpoints। (3) Privacy & Confidentiality = Borrower Records Protected। (4) Democracy & Social Justice = Informed Citizenry, Equal Access। (5) Diversity & Inclusion = Multilingual, Accessible। (6) Education & Lifelong Learning = Library as Learning Centre। इनके अतिरिक्त: Service, Stewardship, Professionalism भी ALA Core Values में हैं। Ranganathan के Five Laws इन सभी Values को Practically Embody करते हैं।
Q6. Embedded Librarian क्या है? यह Traditional Librarian से कैसे अलग है?+
Embedded Librarian = वह Librarian जो Library Building से बाहर जाकर किसी Research Team, Academic Department या Clinical Team का Regular Member बन जाता है। Concept: Barbara Dewey (2004) ने Popularize किया। Types: (1) Academic Embedded Librarian = Research Group में। (2) Clinical Librarian = Hospital में Medical Team के साथ। (3) Corporate Embedded Librarian = Business Team में। Traditional से अंतर: Traditional = Library में Wait करता है Users का। Embedded = Users के पास जाता है — Just-in-time Information देता है। Benefits: Real-time Information Support। Evidence-based Practice। Higher Research Impact। Better Library Advocacy।
Q7. Data Librarian का क्या Role है? FAIR Data Principles क्या हैं?+
Data Librarian / Data Steward = Research Data Management (RDM) में Specialist। Role: (1) Data Management Plans (DMP) Create करना। (2) Open Research Data Publish करना। (3) Data Repositories Manage करना। (4) Researchers को Data Skills सिखाना। (5) FAIR Principles Implement करना। FAIR Data Principles (2016, Wilkinson et al.): Findable = Unique Identifier (DOI)। Accessible = Open Access Protocol। Interoperable = Standard Formats + Metadata। Reusable = Clear License + Provenance। India: INFLIBNET + UGC = Research Data Policies। Government of India Open Data = data.gov.in।
Q8. Professional Skills की 6 Categories कौन सी हैं?+
Modern Librarian की 6 Skill Categories: (1) Technical/Core LIS Skills = Classification (DDC, CC), Cataloguing (AACR2, RDA), Reference Service, Collection Development। (2) IT & Digital Skills = Library Automation (KOHA), OPAC, Digital Library (DSpace), Metadata। (3) Communication Skills = Reference Interview, Presentation, IL Teaching, Multilingual। (4) Management Skills = Budget, HRM, Project Management (PERT/CPM), SWOT, Marketing। (5) Research Skills = Bibliometrics, Scientometrics, SPSS, Citation Analysis, Evidence-based Librarianship। (6) Subject Specialist Skills = Domain Knowledge, Embedded Librarian, Liaison Role। 21st Century में IT + Communication + Research Skills = Most Critical।
Q9. Knowledge Manager के रूप में Librarian क्या करता है?+
Knowledge Manager के रूप में Librarian: Knowledge Management = Organisational Knowledge को Capture, Store, Share और Apply करना। Librarian की भूमिका: (1) Tacit → Explicit Knowledge Convert करना (Nonaka's SECI Model)। (2) Knowledge Base Create + Maintain करना। (3) Communities of Practice Facilitate करना। (4) Intranet/Portal पर Knowledge Organise करना। (5) Best Practices Document करना। (6) Knowledge Audit करना। SECI Model (Nonaka, 1995): Socialisation → Externalisation → Combination → Internalisation। Librarian = KM का Natural Champion क्योंकि Organisation, Classification और Dissemination उनकी Core Skills हैं।
Q10. Privacy Principle को Library Profession में इतना महत्व क्यों दिया जाता है?+
Privacy Library Profession का Core Value इसलिए है क्योंकि: (1) Reading Habits = Personal Choice = Private। (2) Library Records reveal कर सकते हैं — Political Views, Religious Beliefs, Medical Conditions। (3) Government Surveillance से Library को Independent रहना चाहिए। Legal Framework: India = DPDPA 2023 (Digital Personal Data Protection Act)। EU = GDPR 2018। ALA Privacy Policy = Borrower Records = Strictly Confidential। USA: USA PATRIOT Act (2001) ने Libraries को Force किया Records Share करने के लिए = ALA ने विरोध किया। IFLA Code Principle 3 = Privacy & Data Protection। Practical: Library CCTV footage, OPAC search history, Borrower data — सभी Protected।
